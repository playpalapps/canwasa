Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. Here's exactly what Canwasa does — and doesn't do — with your data.

Overview

Canwasa ("the App") is developed and published by Playpal Apps ("we," "us," or "our"). This Privacy Policy describes how information is handled when you use our mobile application, available on iOS and Android. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

Information We Do Not Collect

Canwasa is designed with privacy at its core. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of the following:

  • Personal information (name, email address, phone number)
  • Photos, images, or media files from your device
  • Location data or GPS coordinates
  • Contacts or address book information
  • Browsing history or search queries
  • User accounts or login credentials (none are required)
  • Usage analytics or behavioral tracking data

On-Device Processing

All wallpaper generation in Canwasa is performed entirely on your device. No images, designs, or generation data are uploaded to any server or cloud service. Your creations remain exclusively on your device unless you choose to export or share them yourself.

Third-Party Services

Canwasa uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect certain information for the purpose of serving relevant ads, including:

  • Device identifiers (such as advertising ID)
  • General device information (device model, operating system version)
  • IP address (for approximate geographic region, used for ad relevance)

This data is collected and processed by Google in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy. We do not have access to or control over the data collected by AdMob.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, rent, or otherwise share any user information with third parties. The only data processing that occurs is through Google AdMob as described above, which operates under Google's own privacy policies and terms.

Children's Privacy

Canwasa is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since the App does not collect personal information from any user, there is no risk of inadvertent collection from minors.

Data Security

Because Canwasa does not collect or store personal data on any servers, there is no centralized database of user information that could be compromised. All generated content exists only on your device and is under your control.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this page periodically for the latest information on our privacy practices.
